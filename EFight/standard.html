These are all of the positions in EFight:

  • LLT (Low Level Tester) - Responsible for testing things and giving feedback
  • LLD (Low Level Designer) - Responsible for designing objects
  • HLD (High Level Designer) - Responsible for designing maps and biggger things
  • LLC (Low Level Coder) - Responsible for coding small parts of apps
  • HLC (High Level Coder) - Responsible for coding big parts of apps
  • M (Manager) - Responsible for making sure everything is working well
  • A (Admin) - Responsible for making high level organizational changes
  • HLA (High Level Admin) - Responsible for making sure everything is working, hiring high level people, setting up standards and maintaining the comapny

These are all of the groups in EFight:

  • PV (Popular Vote) - All members, including LLT's
  • WPV (Worker Popular Vote) - All members except LLT's
  • MPV (Management Popular Vote) - All managers, admins and high level admins

These are the standards required for each position:

LLT:

  • Approval from at least 10% of the popular vote

LLD:

  • Approval from at least 10% of the popular vote

HLD:

  • Approval from at least 20% of the popular vote

LLC:

  • Approval from at least 10% of the popular vote
  • Basic understanding of the Go programming language
  • Basic understanding of the 3D and 2D graphics system

HLC:

  • Approval from at least 20% of the popular vote
  • High level understanding of the Go programming language
  • High level understanding of the 3D and 2D graphics system

M:

  • Approval from at least 50% of the popular vote
  • Approval from at least 50% of the management popular vote

A:

  • Approval from at least 60% of the popular vote
  • Approval from at least 60% of the management popular vote
  • Approval from the high level admin

HLA:

NOT CHANGEABLE

These are the powers of the management people:

M:

  • Ability to remove a LLT

A:

  • Ability to remove a LLT
  • Ability to remove a LLD
  • Ability to force remove any non-management person (this power should be used very rarely and can result in firing)

HLA:

  • Ability to remove any non-management person
  • Ability to force remove any person (this power should be used very rarely)
  • Ability to force overturn any decision (this power should be used very rarely)
  • Ability to force hire anyone (this power should be used very rarely)

Regulations:

  • The force remove, hire or overturn ability should be used very rarely. If an admin uses this power they can be removed because of this if 50% of the popular vote agrees. The high level admin can have their decision overturned if at least 90% of the popular vote thinks this was a bad decision.
  • Any intentional creation of bugs or bad things by a coder will result in their instant removal.
