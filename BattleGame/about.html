EFight on GitHub
EFight Battle Game is a free and open source project on GitHub. It is still being developed, but a beta version will be released soon. For more information on the status of EFight Battle Game, look at the status page.

In EFight Battle Game robots have taken over the world and humans have fallen. You are a robot, and you must fight against others robots (other players online) for control over a small town. You will have many weapons available, like lasers that will blow up enemy robots and viruses that will shut down enemy robots. Collect batteries or plug yourself into a power outlet to heal yourself. Robots have infinite lives, but every time you die you lose energy. Every time you kill, you get energy. Use energy to get special powerups and unlock minigames until you reach level 100 and move onto the next town.





Note: Not all features mentioned on this page will exist in the first few releases.
