Welcome to KodMail. KodMail is the mail site of KPlat. Your account info is carried over to KodMail from KPlat. To get started, you can send a message or go to your mail. When you first come here, you probably won't have any mail but you can still look at how at the layout. When you are looking at your mail you can directly send a message. Currently, you may only send a message to one person at a time. Enter the person's KPlat username in the 'to' section, then enter your message.
